A.S. Reliable Transportation Service, LLC is a licensed and insured Non-Emergency Medical Transport company located in Springfield, Illinois. Our goal is to provide a safe, affordable, and reliable option for residents who need to attend important appointments in a timely manner. We are specialize in ambulatory assist and wheelchair transports. We offer 24/7 curb-to-curb and door-through-door services.

We provide service to

Those who need to attend medical appointments.

Children who need to attend school and sporting events.

Impaired and Disabled people that cannot drive.
