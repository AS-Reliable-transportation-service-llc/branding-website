What Makes Us Special

COMFORT

Our fleet of vehicles are designed with you in mind. If you require wheelchair, we have vehicles that come equipped with wheelchair lifts and ramps. This alone can make your journey more convenient and comfortable because you do not have to get out of your wheelchair or struggle to get back into it at your destination. You can simply roll on and roll off.

SAFETY

Our vehicles are well maintained and equipped with both front and rear cameras for the safety of our clients.

RELIABILITY

We are committed to our clients, because we believe that they are the centerpiece of our business. We always make sure our clients are picked up and dropped off at their various appointments in a timely manner.
